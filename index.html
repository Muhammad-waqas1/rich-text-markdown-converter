                What is Markdown?
            

            

                

                    Markdown is a lightweight markup language that allows you to format plain text using simple syntax.
                    It's widely used by developers, technical writers, and content creators for its simplicity and
                    readability.
                

                
Why Use Markdown?

                

                    

                        

                            Easy to Learn
                        

                        
Simple syntax that anyone can master in minutes

                    

                    

                        

                            Universal Support
                        

                        
Supported by GitHub, Reddit, Stack Overflow, and more

                    

                    

                        

                            Plain Text
                        

                        
Readable in any text editor, no special software needed

                    

                    

                        

                            Version Control
                        

                        
Perfect for Git and other version control systems

                    

                

            

        


        
        

            

                Frequently Asked Questions
            

            

                

                    
How do I convert rich text to markdown?

                    
Simply paste your formatted text into the left editor, or use the
                        formatting toolbar to create content. The markdown output will appear instantly on the right
                        side. You can then copy or download the markdown.

                

                

                    
Is this tool completely free?

                    
Yes! This tool is 100% free with no limitations, no registration required,
                        and no hidden fees. All processing happens in your browser for maximum privacy.

                

                

                    
What formatting options are supported?

                    
We support all common markdown features: headers (H1-H6), bold, italic,
                        underline, strikethrough, ordered and unordered lists, links, images, code blocks, blockquotes,
                        and horizontal rules.

                

                

                    
Can I paste from ChatGPT, Word, or Google Docs?
                    

                    
Absolutely! Our converter handles formatted text from any source including
                        ChatGPT, Microsoft Word, Google Docs, Notion, and more. Just paste and watch the magic happen.
                    

                

                

                    
Is my data safe and private?

                    
Yes! All conversion happens entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to
                        any server, and we don't store or collect any of your content. Your data never leaves your
                        device.

                

                

                    
Can I use this for commercial projects?

                    
Yes! Feel free to use this tool for personal or commercial projects. The
                        generated markdown is yours to use however you like.
